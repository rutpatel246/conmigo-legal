Common questions

I can't sign in with Apple / Google

Try the “Continue with Email” option as a fallback. If Apple or Google sign-in keeps failing, please email us the approximate time it happened so we can look at the logs.

My trip data disappeared

During beta, some trip data may be stored on your device only. If you reinstall the app, locally-stored trips may not return. Trips you create going forward will sync to our backend, so the next reinstall should preserve them.

Migo's suggestions are wrong / weird

Migo is AI and occasionally produces inaccurate or generic suggestions. Use the “Update preferences” option in Profile → My Data to improve personalisation. For factual errors (e.g. wrong opening hours), always check the official source before booking.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Account → Delete account. This removes your data from our backend within 30 days.

How do I report a bug?

Settings → Send Feedback inside the app, or email us directly. If you can include a screenshot and a short description of what you were doing, that helps us reproduce the issue.
